About Our Foundetion

We Are In A Mission To
Help The Helpless

Diptimate Foundation (herein after referred to as “DF”) is an emerging NGO registered under section 8 of Companies Act, 2013. It is formed with the intention of growth & development of MSME sector businesses, Agri Entrepreneurs, Agri-Tech businesses & NGO working with same objectives. There are number of schemes from state governments, central government; which are implemented but people at large are not able to take benefit of such schemes, DF will provide the solutions by creating awareness campaigns. DF not only provide the information but solution also how to get benefit of such schemes.
