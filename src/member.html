Membership

Normal Member

It is an annual membership plan. With this membership, you will be given proper knowledge of all such government schemes, which are important for you. If you want to apply under any of such scheme; DF will arrange all required facilities with at least 20% discount.

Prime Membership

It is 3 years membership plan. With this membership, you will be given proper knowledge of all such government schemes. If you want to apply under any of such scheme; DF will arrange all required facilities with at least 20% discount. You will be allowed to join all webinars & seminars free of cost. DF will arrange at least 1 seminar or webinar in a month for your general awareness causing direct or indirect growth of your business, skills, knowledge. You will be allowed for your child counselling at just 50% fees from NGO with field specialist.

Benefits of Membership

  • Platform to interact with the network. “Network is net worth”.
  • Awareness of business about policies & practices. “Knowledge in the key to success”.
  • Government schemes on subsidies & subvention. “Saving of income is earning of income”.
  • Business counselling. “A decision can make you hero or zero”.
  • Personal counselling. “A person personally satisfied can give greater efforts.”
