Agri Entrepreneur

Agriculture is back-bone of India. As we are growing, we are leaving our culture, our life style… we are following western culture activities; but in the mean time with this pandemic on 2020; God has knocked us for being good. Lot of Agri Entrepreneurs are emerged in last few years. Youths have started taking part in agriculture now a days.

DF is giving its plethora for emergence & growth of Agri entrepreneurship.

  • Register Farmer Producer Organization (FPO)
  • Register Farmer Producer Company (FPC)
  • Register Farmer Cooperative Society (FCS)
  • FPC Compliance
  • FCS Compliance
  • FPC BoD / Management Training (personalized)
  • Agri Technology Classes
